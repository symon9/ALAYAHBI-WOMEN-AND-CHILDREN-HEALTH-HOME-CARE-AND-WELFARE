Goal

Our goal is to reduce maternal/Child morbidity and Mortality rates in Rural and hard to reach to Communities of North East of Nigeria and also to assist the less privilege women and Children to have a sustainable skills acquisition

Aims and Objectives

  1. We enhance access to quality Health Care by referring less privileged targeted audiences to Medical Facilities (primary Health Care center/General Hospital) and provide necessary support that they may need.
  2. Raise the awareness on the component of safe motherhood, advocate for communicable and non-communicable diseases awareness, also increase the awareness of immunization 0-18 years and introduce them to self-care practices.
  3. Provide education to less privileged women and Children, which is a powerful driver of development and one of the strongest instruments for removing poverty and improving oneself.
  4. We work towards improving the Socio economic conditions of under privileged women and Children by creating opportunities for a sustainable indigenous skills acquisition which contribute to community development.
  5. We provide food Items, mosquito net,clothes etc to less privilege women and Children towards promoting their health.
  6. Provide data driven insights that guide the development of referral and follow up policies.
  7. We sensitized women on danger of domestic violence and Provide them with necessary support to break free from such harmful situation and also advocate for protection of women's right.
About Us

ALAYAHBI Women and Children Health Home Care and Welfare

Vision Statement

A society where the Right and well-being of women are respected and Children survives beyond their first five years of life.

Mission Statement

To champion initiatives that contribute to healthy lives for women and Children, while equipping them with sustainable indigenous skills that leads to career growth and the overall development of our communities.

Introduction

Alayahbi NGO,is a domiciliary Health Home Care and Welfare working towards the reduction of maternal/Child morbidity and Mortality rates in Rural and hard to reach to Communities of North East of Nigeria, to assist the less privilege women and Children.

We believe in the Health system of North East ,and we are working towards strengthening the referral system/follow up by working with the existing structures in the Six states of North East.

Our activities focus on 5R's:

  • Right place (Rural Community)
  • Right time
  • Right woman/child
  • Right health intervention
  • Right health referral

Our welfare activities engage expertise in indigenous skills acquisition to promote sustainable self-reliance careers.

Thus, we are passionate to give back what will make a real impact in the lives of Rural Women and Children.

Finally, Internally Displaced Persons (women and Children), are not left out in our activities.
